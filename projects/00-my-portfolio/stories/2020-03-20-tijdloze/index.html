Studio Brussel's
De Tijdloze
Every year, Studio Brussel, currently - in 2019 - Flanders' 3rd most listened to radio station and arguably Flanders' most famous radio station for rock and alternative music, puts together a list of world's best alltime songs. The list is voted on by listeners and contains each year 100 songs put together based on people's top 3 all time songs.
