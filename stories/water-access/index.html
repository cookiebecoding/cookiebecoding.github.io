Standard of Living is Improving Worldwide

The standard of living is measured by a set of different metrics. Access to clean water contributes to the standard of living, not only in that it improves people's comfort, but also in that it is directly related to life expectancy, economic growth and levels of poverty. In the past 25 years, tremendeous progress has been made in providing every human on earth access to clean water, as the proportion of the global population not having access, has decreased from 23% to below 9%. However, nonetheless the progress worldwide this remains an ongoing battle - in particular in Africa, where to this day 27% of the population does not have access to clean water.
