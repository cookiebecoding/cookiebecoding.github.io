A Friendship Summarized in 2 Years of Whatsapp Messages

Just like undoubtely many of you have several Whatsapp group chats in which you communicate on a daily basis with your closest friends, so do I. Recently I decided to scroll all the way up in our group chat, only to conclude that there were over 2 years worth of messages in the chat. I couldn't help but wonder how many messages were in there, how it evolved over time, as well as if my messaging behaviour was similar to that of my friends. So I decided to download the entire chat history and analyse the cr*p out of it.
I invite you to ignore all privacy objections my friends will surely have about me posting this analysis to the internet and take a look into me and my friend's personal messages from the past 2 years! (Scroll down to blablabla.)

A summary

  • a group of 7 friends, let's call them Janice, Sharon, Ashley, Joanne, Lucille, Susan and Jessica (they're the best!);
  • 7.973 messages sent;
  • during a period of 1.059 days;
  • of which we talked on Whatsapp 614 days (or 58% of days).

Wine Nations as well are Drinking Less Wine

... but still stick to wine. Similarly to the trend we see for beer drinking in beer countries, the wine consumption in historical 'wine drinking countries' has been decreasing as well. However, with exception of Spain - where since the early 2000's beer takes a more important part in the alcohol consumption than wine - for most wine countries the consumption of beer increases only a little or not at all.
